# Repo Validation Matrix

This matrix intentionally stays small. Hard fails protect only the risks that would materially break the product: wrong founder money math, misleading temporary saves, broken guided usability, missing West Peek branding, internal language leakage, build failure, and unsafe artifact structure.

| Check | Command | Severity | Production Risk | What It Proves | What It Does Not Prove | Failure Handling |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| Founder money math | `node tests/math.test.mjs` | HARD FAIL | Founder could be misled about ownership, payback rights, $0 outcomes, or minimum sale price needed | Priced-round dilution, debt-before-equity, senior payback order, pari passu sharing, investor gets paid twice, caps, growing payback, investor choice between payback/shares, founder-$0 outcome, and target payout threshold | Does not model every possible term sheet clause or legal nuance | Fix formula or downgrade/remove unsupported scenario copy |
| Example reset integrity | `node tests/presets.test.mjs` | HARD FAIL | Built-in examples could drift, mutate, or fail to reset | Industry examples remain immutable and editable copies do not overwrite originals | Does not prove browser clicks | Fix cloning/reset source behavior |
| Temporary saved scenarios | `node tests/session-behavior.test.mjs` | HARD FAIL | Product could falsely imply saved scenarios survive refresh | Saved scenarios are page-lifetime only; no durable browser storage is used | Does not prove browser interaction by itself | Remove durable storage and align copy |
| Founder-facing copy + West Peek brand | `node scripts/validate-ui-copy.mjs` | HARD FAIL | UI could expose internal language, miss required warnings, or ship without approved brand identity | Required warnings/disclaimers exist, forbidden internal terms are absent from app copy, WP logo asset exists, product name is present | Does not prove every visual detail is human-approved | Rewrite user-facing copy or restore brand asset |
| Anti-theatre browser journey | `python3 scripts/run-playwright-e2e.py` | HARD FAIL | Dashboard could render labels but fail real usage | Founder can complete branded guided flow; edit sale price/debt/target; see outcome changes; prove founder-$0 despite ownership; prove investor-paid-twice effect; add/remove round; save temporary scenario; revisit saved scenario; reset to original; clear saves; prove saved scenario disappears after reload; prove mobile path remains usable; produce screenshots | Does not prove deployed hosting, every browser, or every legal financing structure | Fix UI behavior, math, or journey expectation |
| Static build | `node scripts/build.mjs` | HARD FAIL | Artifact may not render as a static app or may omit brand assets | Source files and public brand assets are copied into `dist/` and app can be hosted statically | Does not prove deployed URL | Fix source/build script |
| Visual human review | Generated screenshots in `test-results/` | STRONG WARNING | Branding could be technically present but visually weak | Desktop and mobile screenshots exist for review after E2E | Does not prove owner approval unless reviewed | Review screenshots before public launch |
| Deployed smoke | Not configured for v0.1 | INFO | Public host could differ from local behavior | Nothing in v0.1 | Does not prove deployed runtime | Add when a deployment target exists |
| GitHub Actions | Not configured for v0.1 | INFO | CI could fail after push | Nothing in v0.1 | Does not prove workflow status | Add if repo is connected to GitHub Actions |
